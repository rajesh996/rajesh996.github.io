Cricket

Cricket is the world famous sport and is like a religion when considered in India especially. No different from others of my age, even I started playing Cricket at a very young age and very soon I became a big fan of the sport. As many people I too dreamt of becoming a cricketer and represent my nation at International level. But that didn't happen.


But I started to enjoy the sport and get the pleasure by playing it in my free time with my friends, colleagues and when I was in my college, I coached few students from a Government school near my house in Cricket. They were interested in the sport and they got the techniques in the sport very soon. And now they are finding their way in the state cricket board, by playing in various leagues and by proving themselves.
